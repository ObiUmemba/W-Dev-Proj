My Best Movies for 2023

This is a list of movies I consider interesting from 2023

LEO

This is a well scripted family movie which I enjoyed
watching with my kids. We had loads of laughter and gave
us that atmosphere of happiness.

It had a lot of advice for the kids with regards life
lessons; learning to be brave, having consideration for people,
how to make friends, and believing in oneself.

Extraction 2

This was a really cool movie with lots of action, shot in
21st century settings, captivating, and with a nice story line.

Equalizer 3

This was a typical Denzel Washington movie, interesting from
the beginning till the end. It was an absolute thrill ride which
from the start hooks you with its intense action sequences, riveting
plot, and the enigmatic charm of Denzel Washington's character
